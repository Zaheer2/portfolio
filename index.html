About Me
Crafting iOS Experiences

I'm an iOS Developer with over 10 years of experience specializing in Swift, Objective-C, UIKit, and SwiftUI. I build scalable, high-performance iOS applications with a focus on clean architecture and outstanding user experience.

From Firebase integration and REST/SOAP APIs to CI/CD pipelines and App Store publishing, I bring full-cycle expertise from idea to production.

I've led teams and shipped applications across fintech, telecom, fleet, HR, and productivity domains — including work for Etisalat Group and other enterprise clients.

My approach centers on MVVM, VIPER, and SOLID principles, ensuring every codebase is maintainable, testable, and built to scale.
